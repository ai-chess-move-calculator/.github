## 🤔 What Exactly Is the AI Chess Calculator?

At its core, the AI Chess Calculator is a smart desktop tool that uses powerful chess engine algorithms to assess any position you throw at it. Think of it as a digital analyst that processes positions the way a grandmaster would—only faster. You set up the board, and the AI responds with clear insights: optimal moves, tactical alerts, and positional scores.

It’s built for clarity and speed, with no distractions. Just you, the board, and the cold logic of the machine.

## ⚡ Core Features

- **Instant Position Analysis:** Drop into any board state and get an immediate read on who’s ahead and by how much, using precise centipawn values.
- **Smart Move Recommendation:** Let the AI point you to the most powerful move in any position—perfect for post-game review or training.
- **Multi-Variation Display:** Explore multiple top-tier lines at once. See not just the best move, but the second and third best options too.
- **Adjustable Search Depth:** Want a quick scan or a deep dive? Control how many moves ahead the engine calculates depending on your needs.
- **Full Move Navigation:** Step back and forth through any game to see how positions evolved or test out alternative lines.
- **PGN & FEN Integration:** Load full games or specific puzzles with ease using standard chess notation formats.
